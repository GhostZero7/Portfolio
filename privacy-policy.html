MoTrack
Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026
01_Overview

MoTrack is a personal finance tracking app built for Zambia. It reads mobile money SMS notifications from Airtel Money and Zamtel to automatically track your transactions, spending patterns, and financial insights. Your privacy is the foundation this app is built on.

02_Data_Access

MoTrack requests permission to read SMS messages on your device. This access is used exclusively to detect and parse mobile money transaction notifications from Airtel Money and Zamtel — specifically amounts, dates, and recipient references.

03_Data_Usage
  • SMS messages are read and processed entirely on your device
  • Only financial transaction data is extracted and stored locally
  • Used solely to display transaction history, analytics, and summaries
  • Personal conversations and non-financial SMS are never read or stored
04_Data_Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database. Nothing is transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party. MoTrack has no backend — no account, no remote login, no data sync.

05_Data_Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or transmit your data to any third party under any circumstances. Your financial data never leaves your device.

06_Data_Deletion

You can permanently delete all data at any time from within the app under Settings → Clear All Data. Uninstalling the app also removes all locally stored data.

07_Permissions

READ_SMS — required to detect mobile money transaction notifications. This is the core functionality of the app. Without this permission the app cannot function.

08_Children

MoTrack is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from minors.

09_Policy_Changes

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL with a revised date at the top.
