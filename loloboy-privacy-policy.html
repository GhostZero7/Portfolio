lOlo Boy
Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026
01_Overview

lOlo Boy is a task marketplace platform that connects people who need help with skilled service providers. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information.

02_Information_We_Collect
  • Account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Profile information (profile picture, bio, location)
  • Task details (descriptions, locations, pricing, images)
  • Chat messages between users
  • Ratings and reviews
  • Device information and usage data
03_How_We_Use_Your_Information
  • To provide and maintain the lOlo Boy service
  • To facilitate task posting, acceptance, and completion
  • To enable real-time chat between users
  • To send push notifications about task updates
  • To process ratings and reviews
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security
04_Data_Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, secure authentication, and access controls.

05_Data_Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With other users as necessary for task completion (task details, chat messages, ratings)
  • With service providers who help us operate the platform (Firebase, cloud services)
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent
06_User_Rights
  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of push notifications

To exercise these rights, contact us at musambamwim@gmail.com

07_Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication for secure user login. You can sign in using email/password or Google Sign-In. We do not store your passwords — they are managed securely by Firebase.

08_Push_Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging to send push notifications about task updates, new messages, and important platform announcements. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

09_Children's_Privacy

lOlo Boy is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10_Data_Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

11_Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure Firebase Firestore rules
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication
12_Changes_to_This_Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
